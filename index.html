Consulting Across All Latitudes

Custom GIS Mapping Solutions that Suit Your Business Needs

Layer Larks number one priority is to ensure that we meet all your Geographic Information System needs. Our GIS services can turn boring figures into easily digestible visual representations of spatial information that will ensure your stakeholders can make well informed decisions.

Our approach is simple:

  • Schedule a free 1 hour consultation with us to determine the scope of the project and your data requirements
  • Set an agreeable timeline and choose a team member whose skills match with the project requirements
  • Let us map, model, query and analyse your data to find the answers you need

Industries

Environmental

Our team of experts is well versed in the science of predicting the behaviour and occurance of environmental processes and its related variables. Whether it be a site suitability model, least cost path or visulizing hydrological surfaces, we've got you covered! Our team is also capable of advanced interpolation techniques suited to your project needs. We pride ourselves in our ability to make a difference in the environmental realm.

Healthcare

Allow us to apply our advanced spatial analysis techniques to provide your stakeholders with a communicative visual representation of your key health data. Layer Larks specializes in analyzing spatial distribution and trends, preforming geostatistical analysis and utilizing geocoding and networking. We are also wizards of automation - allow us to automate a workflow thats suitable to moniter trends in your data as needed.

Remote Sensing

Our in depth knowledge of satellite and aerial imagery enables us to assist you with the interpretation and measurement of physical, biological and cultural features. Spectral signatures of targets, photogrammetry concepts, geometric correction and image classification techniques are just a few of our specialities. Allow us to derive meaning from your satellite or aerial imagery that enables your stakeholders to make informed decisions.
