Team Descriptions

Lori Lowy

Owner and Project Manager

Lori's vital role is to host our website and oversee all of the projects that we work on. With Lori at the head of our team, all projects run smoothly and all goals are met. Outside of Layer Larks, you can find Lori searching for vintage clothing and jewelry across the city or leading others in peaceful meditation and stretches at a yoga studio.

Helen Plesko

Team Lead and GIS Consultant

With a teaching background, Helen brings her leadership skills to Layer Larks to ensure tasks get completed and deadlines are met. Outside of Layer Larks, you can find Helen hiking or biking through Ontario's scenic trails or baking some of her favourite chocolatey desserts.

Craig Jurkiewicz

Director of Innovation and Strategy

Craig brings his innovative ideas to each project and ensures that every one of our clients' experiences is top notch. Outside of Layer Larks, you can find Craig teaching his dog new fancy tricks or working towards his fitness goals at a local gym.

Lilit Markarian

Talent Recruiter and GIS Consultant

Lilit is full of life and creativity, and uses her personable skills to search for talent from all over the world to join our team. Outside of Layer Larks, you can find Lilit serving aces on the tennis court and connecting with her artistic abilities through drawing and painting.

Ryan Jeffery

Director of Information Technology

Ryan is quick to offer problem-solving solutions to the team and our clients to ensure all of our goals are met. Outside of Layer Larks, you can find Ryan, A.K.A. the Fungi, growing the rarest of mushrooms at home and maintaining his ant farm.
